The Business department offers professional training to students to enable them to meet the challenges of the economic environment and to be leaders in business and related fields.

Within the Business Administration major, students may elect to emphasize training in Arts Administration, Finance and Accounting, International Business, Management, Marketing, or Sports Management. Beyond this, majors may select from a wide variety of elective courses in order to receive more specific training and to develop skills and expertise.
