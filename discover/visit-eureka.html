Come. Visit Eureka. See this special place for yourself.

Eureka College is located in the City of Eureka, Illinois in Central Illinois. The campus is nearly 70 wooded acres with twenty-four academic and residential buildings, an open-air amphitheater, an arboretum, the Reagan Museum, the Reagan Peace Garden, and impressive new sports facilities.

Driving

The City of Eureka is situated within 20-30 minutes of both Peoria and Bloomington-Normal, Illinois. It is a 2-1/2 hour drive from Chicago and within 3-1/2 hours of Indianapolis and St. Louis.

Flying

Both the Peoria International Airport and Central Illinois Regional Airport in Bloomington, serve Eureka's campus.
