Welcome to Eureka College! On Sunday, January 12, 2014 there will be a mandatory Orientation session for new Eureka College students. The Orientation will include important information for all new students about student life, information technology, financial aid, the library, and college policies, as well as an opportunity to meet some of our current students and fellow new students.

January Orientation will begin at 12:00 pm in Moser Lobby of the Donald B. Cerf Center with Check-in followed by Lunch in the South Terrill Room. Your student ID photo will also be taken during Check-in. Throughout the day you will have the opportunity to attend a variety of informational sessions concluding with an Optional Campus Tour of the academic buildings.

Please R.S.V.P. by Friday, December 20, 2013 with the names of those attending by emailing sjiter@eureka.edu or by calling (309) 467-6420. If you are unable to attend please let us know. The college will be closed for winter break Monday, December 23rd – Wednesday, January 1st. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to ask. We look forward to seeing you in January!

Melick Library was built in 1967 and was named after the brothers Wesley and Clinton F. Melick. It is a valuable and vital resource for the College Community. It houses the Library collection and provides Information Literacy instruction on accessing and evaluating the resources found in its open stacks as well as licensed online databases.
