Congratulations to our graduates joining the alumni ranks! This significant milestone is just the beginning for your lifelong relationship with Eureka College.

Baccalaureate will be held on Friday, May 13, at 6 pm in Becker Auditorium. Baccalaureate is an act of worship in which all graduates, faculty, staff, family and friends are invited to participate.

Commencement is on Saturday, May 14, at 10:30 am in the Christine Bonati Bollwinkle Arena and Convocation Center. The President's Reception for graduates, families, and guests will immediately follow the ceremony and take place in the Terrill Rooms of the Cerf Center. former White House senior advisor to President Barack Obama, David Axelrod, will deliver the 161st commencement address.
