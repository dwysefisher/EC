Hello and Greetings from Eureka College!

We have established a Summer Arts Festival at the College, with programs which offer interest and opportunity for many of your students. Our Festival is in its second year this summer, and includes programs in music (chorus and strings) and theater arts.

Young actors (ages 3 through 13) can have fun growing their skills in the Children's Theatre Camp, June 9-13, with a concluding performance of Winnie the Pooh.

String players in grades 7 through 12 can attend the Youth Strings Camp June 9-14, playing chamber music and string orchestra music - this can be a day or an overnight camp.

The Choral Camp runs from June 15 to June 21, and all students stay overnight in Eureka dorms.

Please consider forwarding this invitation to teachers, so their students can learn about our excellent Arts Camps!

With best wishes,
David Commanday
Director
Eureka College Summer Arts Festival

This summer, we will continue the tradition of offering arts programming for youth and adults in the Central Illinois region. These experiences provide training and individual attention from qualified instructors, as well as opportunities to meet and socialize with other students who possess similar interests.

The Eureka College Summer Arts Festival will offer workshops and camps on stage combat, singing, stringed instruments and children's theatre.

The workshops and camps include (click on the camp name for additional detail on each): 

  • Central Illinois Stage Combat Workshop, May 19-June 6
  • Youth Strings Camp, June 9-14
  • Children's Theatre Camp, June 9-13
  • Summer Choral Camp, June 15-21
  • Chamber Strings at Eureka, June 19-22
