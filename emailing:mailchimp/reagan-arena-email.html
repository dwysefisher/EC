Since Ronald and Neil Reagan helped raise the money for the Reagan Athletic Center more than 40 years ago the Reagan Gym has hosted its share of historic athletic and special events. It is a special place where graduates from the 80s came to listen to their beloved alum and President Ronald W. Reagan speak.

Red Devils flocked to the gym in the 90s to watch several historic teams, none more legendary than the 1994 National Champions led by Head Coach Dave Darnall. Recent events inside the Reagan Athletic Center have included Supreme Court Justice Sandra Day O'Connor, Joe Scarborough and over 250 Red Devil wins since 2000.

The Reagan Center is a special place for EC students, alumni, and friends. These renovations and upgrades will support the viability of the Reagan Center and its' ability to host great campus events for decades.

With this plan in place, construction started almost one month ago today as a Caterpillar Skid Steer stuck its skid forks into the top floor of the Reagan Center beginning the renovation of the Reagan Center.

Here is just a short list of the renovations:

  • A more efficient HVAC System providing climate control 365 days a year, including an air conditioned 2015 Commencement Ceremony.
  • A new state-of-the-art gym floor providing 2 volleyball or basketball courts for classes, intramurals, and intercollegiate competition.
  • Chair back bleachers throughout with a new horseshoe layout. This new layout will create a larger capacity for commencement and special events.

The HVAC system will be completed by commencement this year and the whole project is set to be completed in the fall of 2015.
