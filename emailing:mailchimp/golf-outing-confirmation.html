Thank you for supporting Eureka College Athletics by registering to play in the 2015 Eureka College Golf Outing. I am excited to have you join us for a great day of golf at the Den at Fox Creek in Bloomington, Illinois. Registration will begin at 12 noon with a shotgun start at 1:00 p.m. Dinner, awards, and our auction will immediately follow at approximately 5:30 p.m.

Please note that this year's outing is at a new course, the Den at Fox Creek in Bloomington, Illinois. An Arnold Palmer Signature Golf Course, the Den is a Scottish Links style golf course featuring over 130 sand traps, and water hazards on half of the holes. It should be a great new challenge for the Eureka College Golf Outing.

I appreciate your support of this great event and, more importantly, our student-athletes. The Den is located at 3002 Fox Creek Road in Bloomington, Illinois. I look forward to seeing you on Friday, June 26 for a great day in support of Eureka College Red Devil Student-Athletes!
