Dear {#First Name#},

My name is Mike Sain, and I am currently a Senior Reagan Fellow at Eureka College.

Congratulations on your selection as a semi-finalist! I am excited for you to visit campus for Reagan Weekend on Feb. 19–20. The weekend is packed with a mix of social and professional experiences that will help you make a clear decision about life beyond high school, just as it did for me. My personal experience at Reagan Weekend was the most valuable opportunity that any college offered me to decide where would be the best place to spend the next four years of my life, and choosing Eureka College has hands down been one of the best decisions I have made for myself thus far!

Four years ago around this time, I accepted my invite to attend Reagan Weekend to compete for the Ronald W. Reagan Leadership Program. After spending less than two days on campus during Reagan Weekend, I was exposed to a multitude of new opportunities and ideas which completely changed my view of college life. The chance to be a part of the campus community firsthand, stay in the dorms, make plenty of new friends, and meet some of the most involved and caring faculty and staff, all helped me make the crucial decision that Eureka College was where I needed to be.

Now as a second semester senior in college, I look back on all of the wonderful experiences I've had here, whether it was spending the summer working with the Cleveland Indians or in New York City working with the NBA for my Reagan Program Mentorships, pulling all-nighters with friends to study for exams, or being chosen as Eureka College’s Lincoln Laureate. I’m so glad I picked Eureka College.

If you have any questions about the weekend, check out the details online. Also, the current Reagan Fellows including myself will be calling you a week before Reagan Weekend. We have all been in your shoes, and we want to be a resource to you!

Thanks for your time, and I look forward to seeing you on campus Feb. 19–20. Good luck with your final semester of high school!

Sincerely,
Mike Sain
Ronald W. Reagan Fellow
Class of 2016
Columbus, OH
