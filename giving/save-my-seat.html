The Reagan Athletic Facility Arena & Convocation Center

The Reagan Center revitalization is a critical factor in successfully serving our student athletes, attracting college enrollment, and creating a point of pride for the campus and the communities in the Central Illinois region, who use Reagan Center for numerous activities, sporting events, graduation and summer camps.

The renovation will allow Eureka College to be able to offer the only All-Chair Division III Competition Arena in Illinois and in doing so provide an in-demand venue for major events, including Eureka College Commencement.

New arena highlights & amenities

  • Nearly 1,000 chair-back seats
  • 1,500 seat capacity for commencement and special events
  • Multiple volleyball and basketball courts
  • State-of-the-art wall-to-wall maple flooring
  • VIP balcony observation with easy access, top-loading BleaChairs
  • Court reorientation
  • Air Conditioning
