Charitable Solicitation Disclosures

Eureka College is located at 300 E. College Ave, Eureka, IL 61530-1500. All contributions will be utilized for its educational purposes, programs and activities. Copies of its audited financial statements and the most recent IRS Form 990 may be obtained by contacting Eureka College's Alumni & Development Office at the address listed above or by calling (309) 467-6317. Eureka College is an Internal Revenue Service Code Section 501(c)(3) tax exempt entity, and contributions are fully deductible for Federal income tax purposes to the extent permitted by applicable law.
