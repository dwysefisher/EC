Welcome back students!

Welcome Week

Welcome Week is your introduction to life at Eureka College. Beginning with Move-in Dayon Saturday, August 23, the Residence Life staff and First Year Experience Leaders will help you get acquainted with Eureka College programs, with the campus, and with each other. Our Greek Life community will help unload your belongings will answer questions and assist wherever they can. Through a full schedule of academic, social, service and ceremonial activities, you will begin your life as a Eureka College student.

Need to get connected or have IT related questions?

Visit IT Services (requires Google login)

As a reminder to all students, for most students your password will likely expire sometime in August or September, and WiFi will no longer work. If you experience an issue where the WiFi login page just reloads after logging in, try logging into a lab computer. If your password has expired, the system will prompt you to change it. Lab computers are available 24 hours a day in Ben Major and the Learning Center are are now open. If you still cannot get in, please email the helpdesk helpdesk@eureka.edu or call 6381 from a campus phone.

Looking for a job?

Openings throughout campus with various divisions as well as with Sodexo are available on our Employment page. Visit often to see the most recent postings.
