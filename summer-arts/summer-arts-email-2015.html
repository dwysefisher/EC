Summer Choral Camp

A week-long residential Summer Choral Camp hosted by Eureka College and directed by Dr. Joseph D. Henry. The camp provides campers with a comprehensive and accelerated choral learning experience including participation in both large and small ensembles, voice classes, private voice studio, music reading, music history and music theory.

As part of the curriculum, campers will study and perform the All-State audition pieces as a means to help students prepare for fall IMEA auditions. A maximum amount of individual instruction and small group lessons will be at the core of the program. Enrollment is capped at 60 on a first come, first served basis. Students who cannot attend the Grand Finale Concert on the final day are ineligible to participate and should attempt registration in another year.

A completed registration form and non-refundable deposit of $100 per person is due by May 31, 2015. Make checks payable to Eureka College Summer Choral Camp and sent to:
