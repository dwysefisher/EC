Let your young stars shine in a week long theater camp at Eureka College. At the end of the week all participants will perform in the show "Charlotte's Web", adapted by Joseph Robinette, based on the book by E.B. White.

Schedule

Monday: Audition and Read-thru: students (aged 6–13) will audition in the morning. After a provided lunch, students will be assigned their roles and the entire cast will have a read–thru of the script.

Tuesday: Act I: students (aged 6–13) will work on Act I in the morning. After a provided lunch, the entire cast will work on Act I.

Wednesday: Act II: students (aged 6–13) will work on Act II in the morning. After a provided lunch, the entire cast will work on Act II.

Thursday: Run-thru: students (aged 6–13) will work the entire show in the morning. After a provided lunch, the entire cast will work on the entire show

Friday: Dress Rehearsal and Performance: students (aged 6–13) will work the entire show in the morning. After a provided lunch, the entire cast will have a full dress rehearsal. All cast members have a 5 pm cast call for a 6 pm performance.

Note: Due to the condensed nature of the rehearsal schedule ALL dates and times are mandatory to be eligible to participate in the final performance.
