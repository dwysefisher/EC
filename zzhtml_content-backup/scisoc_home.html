The hallmarks of the Social Science and Business Division are the richness of experiences we offer our students and the sense of new, previously unforeseen opportunities that unfold under the guidance of our professors. Recently, for example, division undergraduates have worked in law firms, presented original research in Sweden, helped senior citizens with their tax accounting, traveled in the footsteps of Lewis and Clark, recommended business plans to major corporations, studied services for the homeless in Savannah, attended scholarly meetings, and produced the local television news. In every class, activity and program of study, our division is committed to helping you identify and achieve your dreams.
