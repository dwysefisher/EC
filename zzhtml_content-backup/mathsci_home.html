Eureka's Science and Mathematics Division offers students an abundance of possibilities: eight majors, six minors, five secondary certification programs, plus a pre-professional programs for students interested in the health sciences. Whichever path you choose, you'll acquire the knowledge, the analytical thinking and observation skills, and the hands-on experience you'll need to either attend a quality graduate school or land a great first job. In the Science and Mathematics Division, you'll find all the elements of a solid education.
