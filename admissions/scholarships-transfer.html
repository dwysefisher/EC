Eureka College offers competitive scholarships for students transferring from Illinois Valley Community and Sauk Valley Community College.

Steven W. Rigazio Scholarship

The scholarship will be awarded to a student who completes his/her second year of college from Illinois Valley Community College, and transfers to Eureka College to pursue their baccalaureate degree.

The scholarship will cover the full cost of tuition at Eureka College (the recipient must apply for all appropriate federal or state grants and awards). The scholarship shall be renewable for a second consecutive year of study at Eureka College, based on satisfactory progress toward a degree.

Criteria:

  • Academic good standing
  • Good character
  • Positive attitude
  • Financial need
  • Recommendation of the President of Illinois Valley Community College

“Work hard, be honest, treat each other with dignity and respect, and have fun.”

~ Steven W. Rigazio

Sauk Valley Community College-Eureka College: Reagan Transfer Scholarship

The scholarship will be awarded to a student who completes his/her second year of college from Sauk Valley Community College, and transfers to Eureka College to pursue their baccalaureate degree.

The scholarship will be in the amount of $2,500 per year. The scholarship shall be renewable for a second consecutive year of study at Eureka College, based on satisfactory progress toward a degree.

Criteria:

  • Academic good standing
  • Good character
  • Positive attitude
  • Financial need
  • Recommendation of the President of Illinois Valley Community College
