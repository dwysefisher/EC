Academic Merit Awards

Because these scholarships are awarded on merit, students do not have to demonstrate financial need to qualify. For incoming freshmen students, academic merit scholarships are awarded on the basis of high school GPA and ACT composite scores.

Calculate anticipated assistance with our scholarship calculator.

Competitive Scholarships

Our competitive scholarship programs include the Ronald W. Reagan Leadership Program, the Disciples Leadership Program and the Durward Sandifer Mentorship Program. These may require a separate application and an on-campus interview or audition.

Engagement Awards

Leadership, service, and faith-based involvement are critical components of Eureka College’s mission statement. Learn more about engagement awards for demonstrated involvement in these areas as well as in the fine arts (art, band, music, piano and theatre).

Grants

Find grant opportunities unique to Eureka College and Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) students as well as federal/state financial aid.

Transfer Students

All transfer students are eligible for academic merit scholarships as well as Engagement Awards and Grants. Eureka College also offers competitive scholarships for students transferring from Illinois Valley Community and Sauk Valley Community College.

International Studies

Eureka College actively encourages students to take advantage of study abroad opportunities and has set aside financial support in the form of tuition grants to help students realize their dreams to foreign travel. The following may require separate steps for application.

Students may apply to study at Lingnan University in Hong Kong (Accounting, Business Administration and Computer Studies majors), Sophia University in Tokyo, Japan, or for the Julia Colorado International Scholarship (students to study a foreign language (preferably Spanish) abroad).

Learn more about study abroad options or contact the Study Abroad Coordinator with specific questions.

Outside Scholarships

Private and outside scholarships may be available from other sources.
