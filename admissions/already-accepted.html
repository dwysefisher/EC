Welcome to Eureka College!

We look forward to having you join our community this school year. We know you have a lot of questions right now, and hope to start answering some of them with this on-line orientation. Here you will find useful information as you begin the transition process at Eureka College.

Please complete and/or return the necessary forms to the Office of Student Programs and Services at Eureka College, bring them with you when you attend a Jump Start event (freshmen) or Transfermation Event (transfers) this summer or Orientation Day in January. The online forms will be emailed to Student Programs and Services, and you will also receive an electronic copy.

I urge you to take time to review all of this information, and to share it with your family. Should you or your family have questions, please do not hesitate to contact me or the Student Programs and Services staff at 309-467-6420.

I look forward to meeting you soon!
Brooke Campbell
Dean of Students
