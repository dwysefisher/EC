To be considered for federal or state financial assistance and certain types of Eureka College financial assistance, prospective students must complete and submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).

FAFSA forms should be submitted electronically as soon as possible after January 1.
How to Fill Out the FAFSA
