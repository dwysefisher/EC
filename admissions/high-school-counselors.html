Frequently Asked Questions About Eureka College:

When should I apply for admission?

We accept applications on a rolling admissions basis from September until August 1 for fall enrollment. For spring enrollment we accept applications until December 15.

What are the admissions requirements?

Freshmen: minimum ACT 17 and grade point average 2.3/4.0

Transfers: minimum transfer grade point average 2.0/4.0

What is the student-to-faculty ratio?

13:1 with an average class size of 17 students

What is the enrollment?

We currently have just over 700 students, aiding in our ability to personalize attention for our students.

What majors, minors and programs are offered?

We offer more than 30 majors, pre-professional programs, and other programs of study.

Does Eureka College offer athletics?

Yes. NCAA III. St. Louis Intercollegiate Athletic Conference (SLIAC)

Men's: baseball, basketball, cross country, football, golf, soccer, swimming, tennis, track & field

Women's: basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, softball, swimming, tennis, track & field, volleyball

Eureka College offers merit based scholarships ranging from $1,000 up to full tuition. Talent based scholarships are available in Art, Music (vocal & keyboard) and Theatre.
