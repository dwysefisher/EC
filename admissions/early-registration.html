New Student Orientation is a journey at Eureka College that begins with admissions, extends through Early Registration Day (first-year students), JUMPstart (first-year students), TRANSFERmation (transfer students), Welcome Week (all EC students) and well beyond the student's first semester. It helps facilitate the transition to Eureka College and prepares students for the educational opportunities and integration into Eureka Collegeâ€™s intellectual, cultural and social communities.

Early Registration Day outcomes:

  • Introduce students to academic divisions & faculty;
  • Begin to foster relationships (peer-peer, faculty-students, peer-FYE leaders);
  • Discuss Applied Learning Opportunities with parents; and
  • Register students for their Fall Semester.
