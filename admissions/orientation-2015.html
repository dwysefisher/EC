New Student Orientation is a journey at Eureka College that begins with your commitment deposit, extends through Early Registration Day (first-year students), JUMPstart (first-year students), TRANSFERmation (transfer students), Welcome Week (all EC students) and well beyond the student's first semester. It helps facilitate the transition to Eureka College and prepares students for the educational opportunities and integration into Eureka College's intellectual, cultural and social communities.

1

Letter of Intent and Deposit

By choosing to commit to Eureka College, you are taking the next step toward your future. We are excited to be part of the transformational growth that awaits you!

Submit your letter of intent and deposit

2

Early Registration

After making your deposit, be among the first to secure your fall class schedule and begin your relationship with Eureka College.

Sign up for early registration

3

JUMPstart

Attend our overnight JUMPstart orientations during the summer months to build a strong foundation for your first year success! All incoming students are encouraged to attend JUMPstart, even if they participated in an Early Registration Day.

Register for JUMPstart

Are you a first-generation college student? First-generation college students are those students whose parents have not completed a four-year college degree. Many smart and talented first-generation students benefit from additional information and support as they begin to adjust to the college environment.

Learn more about our first-generation program

4

Welcome Week

Welcome Week is your introduction to life at Eureka College. Beginning with Move-in Day, the EC staff and First Year Experience Leaders will help you get acquainted with Eureka College programs, with the campus, and with each other—all before you begin your first day of classes.

See the Welcome Week schedule
