Helping you navigate college funding

Eureka College provides millions of dollars of financial assistance every year, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study awards (campus jobs). At Eureka, we have always been committed to making our education available to qualified students. That's why we were the first coeducational college in Illinois – and the third in the nation to admit students of color! Today, our commitment to educational opportunity is visible through our comprehensive program of financial aid, placing a Eureka education with in reach of hundreds of families each year. Eureka administers millions of dollars of financial assistance every year, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study awards (campus jobs). Most our our students – an unbelievable 100% of them – qualify for financial aid. You may too, so be sure to apply using the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). Find more information about completing the FAFSA, click here or on the Applying for Aid (FAFSA) link to the right. To discover how to navigate college loans, funding, and payment options please feel free to contact us.
