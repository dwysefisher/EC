First Destination Study

December 2013–May 2014–August 2014
90% Knowledge Rate / 91% Placement Rate

The term "knowledge rate" defines the percent of graduates for which we have reasonable and verifiable information concerning the graduates' post -graduation career plans. Data was collected via graduation surveys, online surveys, telephone and email solicitations, alumni records, social media research, and input from faculty, staff, and employers.

The term "placement rate" is calculated by dividing the number of students who are employed or continuing their education by the total number of students about whom we have information.
