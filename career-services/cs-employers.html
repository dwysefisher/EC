Career services for employers make your Eureka connection

Eureka College students have over 25 majors from which to choose to study. They also have over 40 organizations, several athletic teams, and several fraternities to choose to contribute to as members and leaders. The Career Services Office also offers students the opportunity to become involved in Internships from their Sophomore year until graduation, in a variety of locations and businesses. We believe that these kinds of opportunities add to the academic experience at Eureka College and make our students the kind of well-rounded, critical-thinking, good team members employers are looking for their corporation.

Students also participate in a number of job search preparation activities - often sponsored or facilitated by local employers. If you would like to become involved in these activities please contact Career Services.

Job, internship, and volunteer postings

Employers may post job notices and internships directly to Eureka College students and alumni via EC Career Link, or contact the Director of Career Services for a recruitment plan tailored to your opportunity.

Expand your Search

The Illinois Small College Placement Association (ISCPA) connects employers with the career centers, students and alumni from 23 colleges in Illinois. ISCPA is best-known for its premier job fair: CareerFest; the free Online Career Center; collaborative interviewing programs including Fall Interview Day; and promoting high-quality career services designed for students and employers.
