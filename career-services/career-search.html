Job Posting Resources

Notice: The following external web links are provided as a convenience to our students. You may use them at your own risk. Eureka College does not sell or endorse the products or services advertised below. If there is a problem with any of this information, please contact its author - not Eureka College.

  • EC Career Link: Site maintained by Eureka College Career Services, with postings available only to Eureka College students and alumni
  • ISCPA Online Career Center: Job and internship postings available only to ISCPA member school students and alumni. Keep "school name" as ISCPA for an accurate search
  • Indeed: In one search, Indeed gives students free access - instantly, in a single search - to millions of jobs and internships from hundreds of job boards, newspaper classifieds, and company websites.
  • US.jobs by the National Labor Exchange: Published through the National Labor Exchange, this site brings together jobs postings from 35+ online job sites.
  • Regional Help Wanted online listings: This is a regional job search site for a variety of positions including bachelor's degree and non-bachelor's degree required.
  • Illinois Job Network - Through this site you can search for jobs (FT, PT, Internships) in cities throughout the state of Illinois.
  • Career Rookie: This site includes intern and entry level job postings as well as links to other valuable career development and planning resources.
  • Career Builder: This site provides search and resume posting options
  • www.CollegeRecruiter.com: This site provide daily job listings and career search articles.
  • www.tech-centric.net: A leading technology job portal, providing students and graduates thousands of daily openings throughout the country, FT, PT, internships and co-op related jobs. There is no cost for using this service.
  • Idealist.org: This web site provides links to over 40,000 nonprofit and community organizations in 165 countries, which you can search or browse by name, location or mission to learn about volunteer, internship, and full-time job opportunities in the non-profit sector.
  • http://jobs.newscientist.com: This web site provides assistance in searching for biology, chemistry & other science jobs.
  • Federal Employment: This site provides a link to employment opportunities available with the federal government.
  • State of Illinois Employment: This site provides resources available for those interested in employment opportunities with the State of Illinois.
