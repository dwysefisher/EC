This event runs concurrently with the Student Leadership Conference, offering the opportunity to mingle with students and later break into informational sessions that will challenge you and connect you with the Eureka Community. During the weekend, we will

  • Inspire your personal growth with a discussion on Identity Capital, led by Dr. Ann Fulop, Associate Professor of Psychology and Past President of the Faculty at Eureka College.
  • Sharpen your skills with sessions on Marketing Yourself, Mentoring Others and Managing Your Environment in relationship to others while collaborating and building on your personal strengths
  • Engage you in conversation with students and alumni
  • Recharge your batteries with delicious food and drink
  • Inform you regarding Eureka events and Initiatives

Mickeisha Turner Armstrong '93

Trustee and Retirement Services Manager, Country Financial

Mickeisha Armstrong is the Retirement Services Manager at COUNTRY Financial in Bloomington, IL. Prior to her current role, she managed the Employment Department at COUNTRY, worked as the Manager of Benefits Administration and HRIS for Mitsubishi Motors of North America and has over 20 years of professional human resources experience.

She has served as a class facilitator for the women and children homeless shelter at Peoria's Southside Mission. Over the course of her career, she has facilitated numerous organizational training classes and has coached employees on how to advance in their career. She was recently a featured presenter for the Benedictine College 2013 Women's Institute for Global Leadership Program and addressed the 2013 Eureka College graduating class during May commencement.

She received a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration from Eureka College in 1993. Having a passion to help others, she serves as a member of the Eureka College Board of Trustees, is board President for the Illinois Heart and Lung Foundation and volunteers at Northwoods Community Church.

Mickeisha resides in Metamora, IL with her Eureka College sweetheart David and their son Jalen.
