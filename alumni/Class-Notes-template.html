60s

Michael Schroeder '74 has relocated to Douglas, AK.

Anne Craig-McEldowney '75 retired as a LCSW Social Worker Type 73 Certificate. She lives in Bloomington, IL.

80's

Bobby Gray '81 is a buyer for State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company. He lives in Bloomington, IL.

90's

John Barberis '90, Madison County Circuit Judge, has announced his candidacy for the Fifth District Appellate Court, based in Mount Vernon. He was elected Circuit Judge in 2014, was in private practice for 18 years and was a part-time assistant state attorney in Madison County for 15 years.

00's

Brandon Weger '05 is director of assessment at Illinois Eastern Community Colleges. He lives in Robinson, IL.

Brian Munson '14 is a physical education teacher/coach for Staunton High School. He lives in Gillespie, IL.

Michelle Lacy '15 is a teacher at Limestone Walters. She lives in Peoria, IL.

Mike Sain '16 was honored by Governor Rauner at The Lincoln Academy of Illinois on Nov. 7, 2015, as one of the top college students in Illinois.
