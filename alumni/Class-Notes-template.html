Lisa Marie Adkins Ryder '05 is the new Director of The Alabama Folk School at Camp McDowell. She lives with her husband, James, at Camp McDowell in Nauvoo, Alabama. Lisa Marie will host an EC Durward Sandifer Fellow in a mentorship this summer at The Alabama Folk School.

He will complete his residency training in at the University of Illinois Hospital & Health Sciences System in Chicago under the combined Internal Medicine/Emergency Medicine specialty.
