60s

Bruce Darnall '66 has written two articles on Paul Molitor, Minnesota Twins manager, and Kyle Gibson, Twins starting pitcher. They were posted on-line by Telling Ministries (Talking Truth through Technology). The articles tell about the players' life and their Christian faith.

00s

Jason Vicich '00 completed his Ed.D. from the University of Arkansas. He successfully defended his dissertation titled "Examining teachers' perceptions and adaptations related to the first-year of implementation of the Common Core State Standards in a rural elementary school in Central Illinois." He lives in Decatur, IL with his wife Jamie '02 and their daughter, Anna.

Sarah Jiter '03 is a college recruiter at the Community College of Aurora in Aurora, CO. She resides in Thornton, CO, with her husband Brady.

Amy Gustin Stroo '03 received her Advanced Paralegal Applications Certificate and began her paralegal career at Godin, Denton & Elliot, PC in Bourbonnais, IL, in September. She resides in Bourbonnais with her husband Tyler '03 and their daughter Gwyneth.

Kouri Marshall '05, Executive Director of Democratic GAIN and Founder of 3K1A Strategies, LLC, will participate in a 14-day political exchange program to Australia as a delegate on a trip organized by the American Council of Young Political Leaders (ACYPL). Marshall will join six other emerging political and policy leaders between the ages of 25–40 from across the United States to explore the governance, politics, and policy making in Australia.
