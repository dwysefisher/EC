90s

Monica Batista '94 is an admissions care center liaison for Seasons Hospice and Palliative Care. She resides in Morton Grove, IL.

Jane Groeper '99 recently joined the staff at the Center for Learning Assistance at Bradley University in Peoria. The CLA helps students with learning disabilities, assists those on academic probation, and coordinates tutoring services.

00s

Kouri Marshall '05 will be traveling to Australia as a part of a seven member delegation representing the United States with ACYPL.

Travis Wilcoxen '05 and his wife Jessa, announce the birth of their son Baron Kevin Elias, born Aug. 11, 2015. Travis is an assistant professor of Biology at Millikin University.

Jayme Zobrist '07 recently earned her Master of Public Affairs with a concentration in Nonprofit Management. She currently works at Just Friends Adult Day Services as Program Coordinator. She and her husband, Tim Dunaway '05 reside in beautiful Columbus, IN.

Brad Polanin '09 is a second year assistant principal at Riverton Elementary School after teaching seventh grade in Riverton for four years. He is also the Varsity Football Offensive Coordinator. He has been encouraged by Byron '97 and Misty Chaney '97 Painter, two alumni and Riverton residents, to give an update in the Maroon and Gold! Go Red Devils!

Korte Knoblauch '10 is a physician assistant for OSF Prompt Care.

Tabitha Cotton Jones '13 and her husband, Paul, announce the birth of their child, Taylor, born on Aug. 25, 2015. Tabitha is a case manager for Chestnut Global Partners.
