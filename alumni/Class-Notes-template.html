50s

Bob Meredith '58 was honored on Feb. 9, 2016, when the gym at ROWVA High School was dedicated to him as "The Bob Meredith Gymnasium." Bob has received many honors in his thirty years of coaching basketball as well as playing. He is in the Eureka Hall of Fame as well as the Illinois Basketball Coaches Hall of Fame, and has coached several all star games.

90's

Cory Dirksen '97 moved to Phoenix, Arizona after being named the Corporate Sales Manager for Barrett-Jackson, the world renowned collector car auction company whose auctions can be seen internationally on Discovery and Velocity.

00's

Gwen Brucker Oyer '00 and her husband Chad and son Bennett announce the birth of their daughter and sister, Phoebe Marie, born on Dec. 30, 2015.

Chris Marchand '05 interviewed fellow alum Jeremiah Gibbs '05 on his first book on the subject of apologetics (defending the Christian faith). Chris is the Headmaster at Aletheia Christian School. Jeremiah is the Chaplain/Director of the Lanz Center for Christian Vocation and Formation at the University of Indianapolis.

Grant Ratcliffe '08 and his wife Colie announce the birth of the daughter, Sloane Carrollyn, born on Dec. 23, 2015. She weighed 8lbs 9 oz and was 20 inches long. They live in Edwards, IL.

Lydia Presley '13 has been offered, and accepted, a position as an incoming Ph.D. student at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln. She will continue to teach English Composition in the English program and is excited to continue her study of Native American literature.
