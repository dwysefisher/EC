  • Gates open at 5:30 pm
  • Buffet available from 6-8 pm

EC Alumni and Friends International Travel Opportunity: Prague, Budapest and Vienna in June 2015

The Alumni Office has announced plans for their next international travel opportunity, slated for June 15–25, 2015. An informational meeting on the Imperial Cities: Prague, Budapest and Vienna will be held at the college in Cerf Center on Thursday, June 19th at 6:30 p.m.

Trip Highlights include

  • Prague, Hradcany Castle, Prague Folklore Dinner and Show, Cesky Krumlov, Vienna, Schoenbrunn Palace, Grinzing Evening, Danube River Cruise, Bratislava, Budapest Matthias Church, Fisherman's Bastion, Szentendre Renaissance Dinner
  • 11 Days, 14 Meals – 9 Breakfasts/5 Dinners
  • Per Person Rates: Double $4,249; Single $5,049; Triple $4,219
  • Includes air and taxes and transportation to and from Chicago O'Hare

Please contact the Alumni Office at 877-892-7823 for more information.
