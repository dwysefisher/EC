Dear Reagan Fellow Alumni,

 

It's February on campus, and as you likely remember, that means a busy time for the College and for all of our Reagan Fellows. Among the activities is the annual Reagan Leadership Program Competition Weekend, February 21 and 22, when we will select the incoming class of Reagans.

 

Because so many of us remember the "Reagan Weekends" of our generations with excitement, we are inviting you back to campus to join the weekend and gather with fellow alumni.

Please consider joining us for Reagan Weekend on Saturday, February 22. The schedule for our fellow alumni is as follows:

 

1:15 pm Alumni Panel for participants and candidates (watch five selected recent alumni as they share their stories)
2-5 pm Campus tours available (visit the new sites and reminisce about the old)
Record your memories (take a moment to capture what the program meant to you)*
  Attend the panel sessions to get to know the current fellows and their experiences:
  2 pm Parent Panel
2:45 pm Current Fellows Panel
4:30 pm Closing Mixer; join the participants and candidates**
5-6 pm Reagan Fellow Alumni Gathering
Held in the Rigazio Conference Room in Cerf Center, we will have an informal chance to catch up, enjoy a glass of wine and savor the traditional chocolate covered strawberries!

 

Come for all or just part of the activities and enjoy a visit to campus on one of our favorite weekends of the year - Reagan Weekend! If you can join us, please RSVP to Shellie Schwanke via email at alumni@eureka.edu or phone at 800-xxx-xxxx.

 

We hope to see you here on Saturday, February 22!

 

Best regards,
Corrie Heck
