60's

Bruce Darnall '66 has written an article titled "Brewers' TV Announcer Tells His Faith Story's Play-by Play" on Milwaukee Brewers' tv announcer, Brian Anderson. It was published on-line by Athletes in Action, the sports ministry for Campus Crusade for Christ. It tells about his life and his Christian faith.  The article can be found at: www.athletesinaction.org/news, then look for General News.

70’s

Teena Zindel-McWilliams ’75 was recently named Coordinator of Curriculum and Grants at Richland Community College, Decatur, IL.

90’s

Tracy Ahrens ’92 had two of her nature poems selected and published in the Prairie Writers Guild 2013 anthology called "From the Edge of the Prairie." The Prairie Writers Guild was founded to encourage, develop and promote writers in Northwest Indiana. The anthology has been published annually since 2004.
