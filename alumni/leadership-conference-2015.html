Building bridges to stronger relationships

The Eureka College Alumni Board and Eureka College Student Leadership Ambassadors have developed the annual Alumni Leadership Conference to bring together an elite group of alumni and students who are leaders in their communities and on campus and desire to build connections with and for your alma mater.

See the schedule at a glance

During the conference, you will —

  • Build your personal network and inspire connections with student leaders
  • Sharpen your skills with sessions on marketing yourself, mentoring others and managing your life
  • Recharge your batteries with fellow alumni and delicious complimentary food and drink

This event runs concurrently with the Student Leadership Conference, offering the opportunity to mingle with students and later break into informational sessions that will challenge you and connect you with the Eureka Community. Your life will be enriched as you learn, connect and grow as a person and as a professional.

The year's conference features the StrengthsFinder assessment. Your online registration will prompt us to send you an online access code to complete the assessment. Bring your profile print out of strengths to maximize your time in the session. Attendees will also receive a copy of the StrengthsFinder 2.0 text, written by Tom Rath.

Keynote Presentation

Based on 40 years of research, StrengthsFinder utilizes the strengths based approach to leadership. The Leadership Conference offers large and small group discussions and case studies to explore leadership styles and problem solving. You will leave with a better understanding of your strengths how to leverage them while working with your team and new connections with EC alumni and students.
