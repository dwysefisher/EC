Homecoming Week

Students will be celebrating Homecoming throughout the week in the Race for Old Elm. Remember these great events? Come on out to campus and enjoy these events again with old friends and alongside our current student body.

September 28–October 4

Eureka College homecoming activities with the theme "Superheroes: Unleashing the Power of You" will take place September 28 through October 4.

Sunday 9/28, 5p: Panel Painting (Burgess Lawn)

A panel-painting competition involving student organizations will take place at 5p on the lawn at Burgess Memorial Hall on College Avenue.

Monday 9/29, 6p: Cook-off (Terrill Rooms)

Tuesday 9/30, 6p: Lip Sync Competition & Entertainment Night Practice (Becker)

Wednesday 10/01, 7p: Lip Sync Competition (TBD)

A Lip Sync competition will take place at the Cerf Center.

Thursday 10/02, Homecoming Book Sale from 2-11p, 7p: Entertainment Night (Becker) and 7:30p: "You're a Good Man Charlie Brown" performance (Pritchard Theatre*)

Homecoming Weekend

Come Home to Eureka College to enjoy a weekend filled with fun and memories. Relish in all the traditional homecoming events that you remember so well. Bring the family and experience Eureka College at its best!

9a–4:30p: Book Sale (Melick Library)

Featuring the 2014 Outstanding Young Alumna Award recipient, Anna Freidinger Palmer '06 and the announcement of homecoming royalty.
