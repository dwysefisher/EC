Catherine Kouns Born '83 recently changed jobs and is now the Human Resources Director for the Allen County Board of Developmental Disabilities in Lima, OH.

00s

Brian Ingrassia '01 started a new position as assistant professor of history at West Texas A&M University, near Amarillo, TX, in August 2015. His book, "The Rise of Gridiron University: Higher Education's Uneasy Alliance with Big-Time Football" will be published in a paperback edition by the University Press of Kansas in December 2015.

Jenny Haas Evans '05 is a first grade teacher at El Paso-Gridley Unit #11.

Jillian Rebmann Rosenberger '06 is a Children's Librarian for the Peoria Public Library.

Dan Cameron '09 is a registered PGA apprentice–teaching professional at ALdeen Golf Club in Rockford, IL, a top 50 public golf course in the state.

Jared '10 and Megan Daniel '12 Byard are proud to announce the birth of their son, Kaden Douglas, born on Aug. 7, 2015.

Katie Huizinga Ritchie '10 is a 7/8 Grade ELA teacher for Oglesby School District 125.
