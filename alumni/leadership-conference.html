You are a Eureka College Alumni Champion!

You are a leader in your community and in the eyes of those in your sphere of influence – and your alma mater is proud of you! To celebrate this, the Eureka College Alumni Board and Eureka College Student Leadership Ambassadors have developed this conference to bring together an elite group of alumni and students that are leaders in their communities and on campus and desire to build connections with and for their alma mater, Eureka College.

This event runs concurrently with the Student Leadership Conference, offering the opportunity to mingle with students and later break into informational sessions that will challenge you and connect you with the Eureka Community. Your life will be enriched as you learn, connect and grow as a person and as a professional.

During the weekend, we will

  • Inspire your personal growth with a discussion on Identity Capital, led by Dr. Ann Fulop, Associate Professor of Psychology and Past President of the Faculty at Eureka College.
  • Sharpen your skills with sessions on Marketing Yourself, Mentoring Others and Managing Your Environment in relationship to others while collaborating and building on your personal strengths
  • Engage you in conversation with students and alumni
  • Recharge your batteries with delicious food and drink
  • Inform you regarding Eureka events and Initiatives
