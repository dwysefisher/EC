Friday, April 10

6pm Golden "E" 50th Anniversary Dinner
Gammon Room, Melick Library

Members of the Class of 1965 will gather with their classmates for this event dedicated to 50 years of memories.

Derer Lanes, 1464 US Highway 24 E., Eureka

Come on out to the Eureka Bowling Alley Back Room for a casual evening of fun, catching up with old friends while welcoming the senior class of 2015 into the ranks of EC alumni. (All Alumni & Friends Welcome!)

Saturday, April 12

7–7:40am Run with Reagan Registration
Alumni Tent, Corner of Burgess Street and Reagan Drive

The Alumni Office presents "Run With Reagan" an exciting and healthy way to kick-off the Saturday morning of Alumni Weekend! Proceeds from this event will go directly to the Ronald W. Reagan Society of Eureka College as well as the Alzheimer's Association. Cost is $30 and includes race entry, t-shirt, and post-race pancake breakfast.

Dickinson Commons on College Avenue

Corner of College Ave. and Burgess St.

9:45am Campus Walk-about

Becker Auditorium, Cerf Center

Dr. Loren Logsdon, Professor Emeritus of English, returns with more stories from the tapestry of alumni experiences and memories that capture the spirit of Eureka College.

Noon Experience Eureka Luncheon: Eureka's Visit to the USS Ronald Reagan (CVN–76)
Terrill Room, Cerf Center

Twice, the Reagan Society of Eureka College has been invited by the United States Navy to send a de'egation to participate in the Distinguished Visitor Program, being flown onto and spending a day on a working aircraft carrier during war games at sea. Join alumni and friends who will share their experiences aboard the USS Ronald Reagan (CVN-76) and the USS Carl Vinson (CVN-70). Buffet lunch: $15 per person. Advance registration requested.
