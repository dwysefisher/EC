The Alumni Leadership Connections Conference

The Alumni Development Office will host the 4th Annual Alumni Leadership Connections Conference on January 31 and February 1, 2014. The conference will run concurrently with the Student Leadership conference offering the opportunity to mingle with students and later break into informational sessions that will challenge you and connect you with the Eureka Community. Dr. Logsdon's Studs Terkel Project Spotlight

Significant and Fascinating Events and Moments in EC History Fred Altman's Story of the Battle of the Ivy on Pritchard.

Dr. Loren Logsdon, Professor Emeritus of English, has captured the spirit of Eureka College history in a special project he fondly calls the "Studs Terkel Project." The project is a collection of alumni memories and stories which weave a rich tapestry of the Eureka College experience during the '40s, '50s and '60s.

This collection of stories is available in the Online EC Bookstore .

Basketball Alumni Appreciation Events Planned for February 2014

The Eureka College Alumni Office will host Basketball Alumni Appreciation Events in February 2014 to honor former players and coaches in conjunction with their home games. Alumni from the Men's program will be celebrated on Saturday, February 8 during half-time of the men's game and following the game with a party in Rigazio Lobby of Reagan Center. March Madness Alumni and Friends Gathering

Eureka College will host an alumni and friends gathering on Friday, March 14 from 5 p.m. until 6:30 p.m. at Kelleher's Irish Pub and Eatery in Peoria. This event will take place between Session I and II of the IHSA Boys Basketball Tournament at the Peoria Civic Center. Come visit with Eureka College Head Coach Chip Wilde, Legendary Coach Dave Darnall, and several members of the Alumni and Athletics staff! Whether you are attending the State Tournament or just wrapping up a work day, don't miss this opportunity to enjoy some great fellowship, food, and fun! Kelleher's is located at 619 SW Water Street, Peoria.
