Why I Give
Matt Kennell '04

My Eureka College experience, particularly the professors who took me in, educated me, and helped teach me what it means to be both a caring and productive human being in this world, is without one of the biggest reasons that I am who I am today.  Without them, I cannot see how I would have ever reached the place that I am at today; of this I have no question. The thought that my gift to Eureka College will help them to continue giving other students this same opportunity is why I give to Eureka today.
