The Alumni Outstanding Achievement Award

The Alumni Outstanding Achievement Award recognizes alumni of Eureka College for a single or multiple acts of outstanding achievement in their profession or in service to his or her community or for a sustained period of outstanding achievement at least 16 years from graduation.

 

The Alumni Award of Merit

The Alumni Award of Merit exists to recognize lifetime excellence in one’s profession or service to his or her community.  Up to four Awards of Merit may be given in a calendar year.  An alum may only receive the Award of Merit once in their lifetime.

 

The Outstanding Young Alumni Award

The Young Alumni award exists to recognize excellent in one’s profession or service to his or her community within 15 years of graduation.

 

Eureka College Honorary Alumnus (The Honorary Alum Award)

The Honorary Alumni award exists to recognize a non-student who has provided great effort in the advancement of Eureka College, its students and/or mission. 

 

The Alumni Board President’s Award for Service

The Award for Service recognizes dedication of one’s time and effort to the advancement of the college.  It may be awarded to any individual, including, but not limited to, alumni of Eureka College. 

  

You may nominate yourself or any worthwhile person as set in the guidelines for each award.
