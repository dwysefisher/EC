Welcome Home Alumni!

It's time to celebrate the arrival of Spring and Eureka's Alumni Weekend! And do we have a lot to celebrate here 'neath the Elms. Alumni Weekend has a little something for everyone! This year's highlights include: 50 Year Reunion Celebration of the Class of 1965* and the 4th Annual Run with Reagan 5K Race & 1 Mile Walk and a special presentation by Professor Loren Logsdon.

A special "Experience Eureka" Luncheon is planned for Saturday where alumni and friends will share their experiences aboard the USS Ronald Reagan (CVN-76) and the USS Carl Vinson (CVN-70). Tickets are $15 for alumni faculty & staff $7.50 students.

*Celebration Dinner tickets $25 per person $40 per couple.

This Year's Honorees Include:

  • Honorary Alumnus: Dr. Wesley Phelan,Retired Professor of Political Science
  • Honorary Alumna: Ms. Carolyn Shepherd
  • Alumni Award of Merit: Mr. Bill King, '71
  • Alumni Outstanding Achievement Award: Mr. Rodney Gould, '87
  • Outstanding Young Alumni Award: Dr. Elaine Flores Welch, '00
  • Alumni Board President's Award for Service: Mr. & Mrs. Ernie & Barb Potter, '65
