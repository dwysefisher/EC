Welcome to Reagan Weekend and
Ronald W. Reagan Leadership Competition

Semi-finalist candidates selected to compete for the Reagan Fellowship have been notified by the Eureka College Dean of Admissions by mail. The College requires that semi-finalists take part in the on-campus competition at Eureka College on Saturday, February 21, 2015. Candidates are invited to arrive on campus Friday, February 21 to take part in the social and entertainment activities scheduled. All candidates are strongly encouraged to stay overnight in on-campus housing to gain a full understanding of the Eureka experience.

The competition day will include an interview and a group project in addition to the informal time to get to know candidates better.

The competition will begin with an orientation for all candidates and their families, as well as interviews with candidates and a small group project with candidates. While candidates participate in the competition day, family members are invited to attend discussion groups on campus life. Candidates and their families will also have the opportunity to meet with administrators, faculty, staff, and students.
